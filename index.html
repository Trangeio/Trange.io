Why should one learn?

Let's look at this in two ways. One of them being, On physiological level, learning new things is good for your brain. Learning new skills stimulates neurons in the brain, which forms more neural pathways and allows electrical impulses to travel faster across them. Secondly, Getting a job in this highly competitive market is not as easy as it sounds. So, One needs to Stand Out, which can only be done by learning and adapting to the requirements out there.
